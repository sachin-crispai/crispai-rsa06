### New Product Announcements

SentinelOne has unveiled several new AI security offerings at RSA Conference 2026, focusing on both securing AI systems and leveraging AI to enhance security operations:

*   **Prompt AI Agent Security:** This new offering provides a real-time discovery and governance control plane for AI agents and agentic workflows. It monitors, controls, and enforces policies on agent interactions at machine speed, offering full visibility, risk assessment, and policy enforcement across Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers. It can also automatically remediate unauthorized agentic behavior.
*   **Prompt AI Red Teaming:** Designed for security and product teams, this tool enables the rigorous testing and fortification of homegrown and first-party AI applications. It simulates real AI attacks, such as prompt injections, jailbreaks, privilege escalation, and data poisoning, and provides continuous evaluation as models evolve.
*   **AI data pipelines inside Singularity AI SIEM:** These pipelines are designed to reduce data noise by up to 80% before ingestion, thereby lowering infrastructure costs and enabling AI-driven detection and response across third-party data at enterprise scale.

### Purple AI Updates

SentinelOne's Purple AI, a defining agentic AI security analyst offering, has received significant updates:

*   **General Availability of One-Click Auto Investigation:** Natively integrated into the Singularity™ Platform, this new capability allows analysts to launch complete, agentic investigations with a single click. Purple AI autonomously gathers cross-stack evidence, synthesizes threat data, and constructs comprehensive attack timelines in real time, delivering explainable verdicts and triggering closed-loop remediation via Singularity Hyperautomation.
*   **High Adoption Rate:** Purple AI was included in over 50% of all SentinelOne licenses sold during the fourth quarter of FY26, indicating its significant adoption in real-world SOCs and customer environments.

### Key Differentiators

SentinelOne highlights several key differentiators at RSA Conference 2026:

*   **AI-Native Platform:** The Singularity™ Platform is built on an AI-native foundation, bringing together automation, intelligence, and unified protection to provide security teams with a decisive operating advantage.
*   **Dual Focus on AI Security:** SentinelOne's offerings cover both securing AI systems themselves and utilizing AI to automate and transform security operations.
*   **Securing Autonomous Agents:** The company provides capabilities to secure autonomous agents, offering real-time visibility, enforcement, and governance across GenAI, AI agents, and applications.
*   **Agentic AI in SOC Workflows:** SentinelOne's agentic AI is designed to work within Security Operations Center (SOC) workflows, prioritizing real threats, reducing noise, and accelerating investigations.
*   **Data Noise Reduction:** The new AI data pipelines significantly reduce data noise before ingestion, improving the efficiency of security operations.
*   **Holistic End-to-End AI Security:** SentinelOne emphasizes a comprehensive approach to securing AI, from discovery and governance to red teaming and automated investigations.
*   **Support for Regulated Environments:** New capabilities are introduced for on-premise, air-gapped, regulated, and sovereign environments, enabling highly regulated organizations to adopt AI-driven security.
*   **Leadership Perspective:** SentinelOne's CEO and co-founder, Tomer Weingarten, is delivering a mainstage keynote on the future of defense, operations, and leadership in cybersecurity.
