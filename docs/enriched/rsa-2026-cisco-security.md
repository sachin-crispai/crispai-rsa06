#### New Product Announcements & Updates

Cisco has made significant announcements at RSA Conference 2026, primarily centered around securing AI agents and enhancing security operations.

**Zero Trust for AI Agents:**
Cisco is extending its Zero Trust framework to AI agents, a foundational announcement for the agentic era. This includes:

*   **Agent Identity Management:** Capabilities in Cisco Identity Intelligence and Duo IAM allow organizations to register AI agents, map them to human owners, and enforce granular, time-bound permissions.
*   **Model Context Protocol (MCP) Policy Enforcement:** Cisco Secure Access now includes MCP policy enforcement and intent-aware monitoring to govern agent behavior in real-time, addressing visibility gaps in non-human identities and agent-driven workflows.
