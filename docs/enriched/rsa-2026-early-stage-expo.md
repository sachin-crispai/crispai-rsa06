RSA Conference 2026 is taking place from March 23-26, 2026, at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, California. The conference theme for 2026 is "The Power of Community".

### Conference Overview

The conference spans multiple levels across the Moscone Center (North and South) and the YBCA Blue Shield of California Theater.

**Key Differentiators and Themes:**
*   **Actionability:** A central theme emphasizes "actionability" as the key differentiator in cybersecurity, moving beyond mere visibility to proactive, coordinated actions driven by truthful data.
*   **Agentic AI:** The proliferation and security of agentic AI are major focal points, with many new products and discussions centered on AI-driven defense, autonomous agents, and securing AI applications and workflows.
*   **Community and Collaboration:** The overarching theme, "The Power of Community," highlights the importance of diverse perspectives, knowledge sharing, and collaboration within the cybersecurity industry.
*   **Identity as Cornerstone:** Identity continues to be a foundational element of security strategy and Zero Trust architectures.
*   **Governance:** AI governance, in particular, has emerged as a strategic imperative.

### New Product Announcements

Several companies have announced new products and capabilities at RSA Conference 2026, with a strong emphasis on AI and agentic security.
