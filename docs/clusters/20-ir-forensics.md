## Technology Landscape

### Incident Response Services
Expert-led containment, eradication, and recovery for major breaches.
- **Leaders:** Mandiant, Kroll, CrowdStrike Services, Secureworks, Palo Alto Unit 42

### Digital Forensics (DFIR)
Collect and analyze evidence: memory dumps, disk images, network captures, logs.
- **Tools:** OpenText EnCase, Magnet Axiom, Volatility (memory), Autopsy (open-source)

### Ransomware Response
Specialized negotiation, payment facilitation, decryption, and recovery services.
- **Leaders:** Coveware, Kroll, Mandiant, GuidePoint

### Cyber Recovery / Resilience
Immutable backup, clean-room recovery, business continuity after ransomware.
- **Leaders:** Rubrik, Veeam, Cohesity, Commvault

### Malware Analysis
Static and dynamic analysis of malware samples — sandboxing, reverse engineering.
- **Tools:** Any.run, Cuckoo, Joe Sandbox, CrowdStrike Falcon Sandbox, VMRay

### Anti-Ransomware Platforms
Dedicated platforms to prevent, detect, and recover from ransomware specifically.
- **Emerging:** Halcyon, Illumio (segmentation to contain spread), Rubrik

## Key Questions to Ask at Booths
- Average dwell time in your IR engagements — how long before detection?
- Ransomware: to pay or not to pay — what's your policy guidance?
- Cyber insurance: how do you work with insurers during an incident?
- Cloud IR: how do you handle forensics in ephemeral cloud environments?
- Retainer model: what's included? How fast is the response SLA?
