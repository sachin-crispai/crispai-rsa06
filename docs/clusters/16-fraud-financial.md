## Technology Landscape

### Behavioral Biometrics
Analyze HOW a user interacts — not just WHO they are — to detect account takeover.
- **Leaders:** BioCatch, NeuroID, Sardine

### Payment Fraud Detection
Real-time ML scoring of transactions to block fraudulent payments.
- **Leaders:** Feedzai, Sift, Kount, Stripe Radar

### Digital Identity Verification
Verify real-world identity at onboarding — document check + liveness + database lookup.
- **Leaders:** Socure, Jumio, Onfido (Entrust), IDEMIA

### Account Takeover (ATO) Protection
Detect credential stuffing, session hijacking, social engineering-driven ATO.
- **Leaders:** Sift, BioCatch, Kount, Arkose Labs

### AML (Anti-Money Laundering)
Transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reports, KYC compliance.
- **Leaders:** NICE Actimize, Feedzai, Unit21, ComplyAdvantage
