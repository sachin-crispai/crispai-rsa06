## Technology Landscape

### Enterprise GRC
Policy management, risk registers, control testing, audit management at scale.
- **Leaders:** ServiceNow GRC, MetricStream, Archer, IBM OpenPages

### Compliance Automation
Continuous control monitoring, automated evidence collection, trust reports.
- **Leaders:** Vanta, Drata, Secureframe, Hyperproof

### Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM)
Assess and continuously monitor vendor/supplier security risk.
- **Leaders:** ServiceNow, Archer, ProcessUnity, BitSight, SecurityScorecard

### Board / Executive Reporting
Translate technical risk into business language for board and C-suite.
- **Leaders:** Diligent, LogicGate, Balbix, Safe Security (SAFE)

### Regulatory Compliance (NIST, ISO, SOC, FedRAMP)
Map controls to multiple frameworks simultaneously.
- **Leaders:** Vanta, Drata, Hyperproof, Tugboat Logic

---

## Key Questions to Ask at Booths
- Multi-framework: how do you handle overlapping controls across SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST CSF?
- AI-driven risk quantification: FAIR model integration?
- How do you handle continuous compliance vs. point-in-time audits?
- Board reporting: what metrics resonate with non-technical audiences?
- Integration with security tools (SIEM, vuln scanners) for automated evidence?
