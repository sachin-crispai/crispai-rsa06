## Innovation Themes at RSAC 2026

Based on Early Stage and Innovation Sandbox trends:

### Agentic AI Security
- AI agents that autonomously investigate, triage, and remediate security incidents
- LLM-powered SOC copilots
- **Watch:** Geordie AI, Unbound AI

### Identity for Non-Human Identities (NHI)
- Securing AI agents, service accounts, API tokens — not just human users
- **Watch:** Aembit, Astrix Security

### Browser Security
- Secure enterprise browser and browser isolation
- **Watch:** Island (Gold Sponsor), Talon (acquired by Palo Alto), SquareX

### Post-Quantum Cryptography
- Preparing for quantum-safe encryption — NIST PQC standards
- **Watch:** PQShield, SandboxAQ, Crypto4A

### AI-Native GRC
- Replace spreadsheet-driven compliance with AI-automated continuous assurance
- **Watch:** MyCISO (ESE-28), Vanta, Drata

### ESE Capture Template
For each booth photo taken, record:
- Company name + kiosk number
- One-line problem they solve
- Differentiator vs. incumbent
- Funding stage / notable investors
- Follow-up action

---

## Key Questions for Startups
- What incumbent are you replacing or making obsolete?
- What's your deployment model — SaaS only, on-prem option?
- Integration story — which platforms do you connect to natively?
- Funding stage — seed, Series A/B? Any strategic investors (CrowdStrike Falcon Fund, Cisco Investments)?
- Design partner / early customer reference?
