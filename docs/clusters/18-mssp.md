# Cluster 18 — Managed Security Services (MSSP / MDR)

**Stack:** Outsource detection, response, and operations to 24/7 security experts.

MSSPs and MDR providers supplement or replace in-house SOC teams — especially critical for organizations without the resources to hire and retain security talent.

## Technology Landscape

### MDR (Managed Detection & Response)
24/7 monitoring, threat hunting, and incident response — human + technology blend.
- **Leaders:** Arctic Wolf, CrowdStrike Falcon Complete, Expel, Rapid7, Deepwatch

### Traditional MSSP
Manage security infrastructure (firewalls, SIEM, patching) on client's behalf.
- **Leaders:** Optiv, AT&T Cybersecurity, Secureworks, Trustwave

### Security Advisory & Consulting
vCISO services, security program development, risk assessments.
- **Leaders:** GuidePoint, Optiv, Kroll, Coalfire, Booz Allen Hamilton

### Cloud-Native SOC
MDR services purpose-built for cloud workloads — AWS/Azure/GCP native.
- **Leaders:** Expel, Arctic Wolf, Rapid7

---

## Key Questions to Ask at Booths
- MDR SLA: what's the guaranteed response time from alert to analyst engagement?
- Transparency: do you share your detection logic / playbooks with customers?
- Co-managed vs. fully managed: can customers retain some response authority?
- How do you handle major incidents — dedicated IR team or same pool?
- Pricing model: per endpoint, per user, per log volume, or flat fee?
